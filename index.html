How it works

Three taps to your best card

No spreadsheets. No bank portals. A fast lookup built around your actual wallet.

STEP 01
💳

Select your cards

Choose the cards you actually carry in Settings. Your selection is saved locally — nothing leaves your device.

STEP 02
🔍

Search a store or category

Type any store name — Costco, Shoppers Drug Mart, Shell — or pick a spending category like Groceries or Travel.

STEP 03
🏆

Swipe the winner

Your cards rank by multiplier or cents per dollar earned. The best card is always on top — no mental math required.

FAQ

Common questions

Which Canadian credit card earns the most rewards on groceries?

It depends on which cards you hold. The American Express Cobalt typically earns 5× on groceries and dining; the Scotiabank Gold American Express earns elevated rates at eligible Canadian grocery stores. CrediQuest ranks your specific wallet so you always see which of your cards comes out on top. Always verify current earn rates directly with your card issuer — rates can change at any time without notice.

Is CrediQuest free to use?

Yes, completely free. No subscription, no sign-up, no ads. Open it in your browser or install it as a Progressive Web App on your phone for instant access at checkout.

How do I maximize my credit card rewards in Canada?

The most effective strategy is card segmentation — using a different card for each spending category. CrediQuest automates the lookup so you never have to memorize each card's earn rates. This is general informational guidance only and does not constitute financial advice. Consult a qualified financial advisor for personalized recommendations.

Does CrediQuest store any of my personal information?

CrediQuest only stores which card names you have selected — not card numbers, expiry dates, PINs, or any account details. That selection is saved exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device and is never transmitted externally. See the Legal & Disclosures section below for full details.

How accurate is the rewards data?

Data is maintained on a best-effort basis using publicly available card issuer information. Earn rates are subject to change by issuers at any time without notice. CrediQuest makes no guarantee that displayed rates are current or complete. Always confirm rates directly with your card issuer before making any spending decisions.

Can I install CrediQuest on my phone?

Yes. On Android, tap the install prompt that appears in the app. On iPhone, open CrediQuest in Safari, tap the Share button, then select "Add to Home Screen." It works like a native app with no App Store required.

What if my store isn't in the database?

Use the Contribute tab inside the app to submit a missing store. You can also search by spending category to immediately find the right card for any merchant not yet in the database.
